What is TsuMi?


TsuMi as already mentioned means SendMe, TsuMi is an errand platform that runs daily errands for busy people who can't make it to certain places due to their daily busy schedules, TsuMi connects busy people in need of help with trusted local Taskers who can lend a hand with everything from home repairs to errands. As a Tasker, you can get paid to do what you love, when and where you want — all while saving the day for someone in your city.
